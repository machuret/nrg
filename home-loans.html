First home buyers

NSW grants and schemes, minus the jargon.

If you’re buying your first home in NSW, there’s real money on the table. We’ll check what you qualify for and build it into your plan.

First Home Owner Grant (New Homes)

A one-off grant for eligible buyers building or buying a brand-new home under the value cap. We’ll confirm whether your purchase fits and help you claim it.

First Home Buyers Assistance Scheme

Stamp (transfer) duty exemptions or concessions for eligible first home buyers below the thresholds — often the biggest saving of the lot.

First Home Guarantee

A federal scheme that can let eligible buyers purchase with a smaller deposit and skip Lenders Mortgage Insurance, subject to places and caps.

Heads up: grant and scheme eligibility, value caps and thresholds change and depend on your circumstances. The figures and rules are set by Revenue NSW and the federal government — we’ll check the current detail against your situation before you rely on anything.
