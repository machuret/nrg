In plain English

How consolidation works.

Debt consolidation rolls your higher-interest debts — credit cards, a car loan, a personal loan — into your home loan, or into one new loan. Instead of juggling four repayments at four different rates, you’ve got one, usually at a much lower home-loan rate.

The win is two-fold: your repayments are simpler to manage, and because home-loan rates are typically far lower than card or personal-loan rates, your total monthly outgoing often drops too.

It’s not about getting out of trouble — most of our clients aren’t in any. It’s about seeing the full picture and making your money make sense again. We’ll be honest about the trade-offs too (stretching a short debt over a long term has a cost), so you go in with eyes open.
