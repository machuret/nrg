Tim’s story

Calm under pressure. Honest about the numbers.

I’ve spent my whole working life around lending and most of my life between Newcastle and Sydney. I know these suburbs — the Central Coast, Lake Macquarie, the Hunter — because I live here, shop here, and my kids grew up here.

Before I got into broking I served in Fire & Rescue. You learn pretty quick how to stay calm, work the problem, and look after people when things go sideways. That training never really leaves you, and honestly it’s the same job — someone’s in a stressful spot, and your job is to sort it out properly.

So that’s how I broke. I’ll sit down with you, work out where you’re actually at, and find a loan that fits your life — not just whatever pays the most commission or looks sharpest on a comparison site. Then I’ll still be here when your situation changes, because it always does.

I’m also a Justice of the Peace, which is just another way of saying people round here trust me to do the right thing. Large enough to represent you, small enough to know you. That’s not a slogan — it’s the whole point.

The credentials, for the record. Australian Credit Licence 384496 · AFCA Member 44198 · FBAA Accredited · Justice of the Peace (NSW) · MBA · Professional Indemnity Insured. Happy to talk through any of it — but you probably care more about whether I’ll look after you, and I will.
